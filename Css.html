The Eiffel Tower

About the Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower was designed by Gustave Eiffel and was completed in 1889 for the Exposition Universelle (World's Fair). Standing at 324 meters tall, it was the tallest man-made structure in the world until the completion of the Chrysler Building in New York in 1930.

Interesting Facts

  • Over 7 million people visit the Eiffel Tower annually.
  • The Eiffel Tower is painted every seven years to protect it from rust.
  • The tower was originally criticized by many artists and Parisians for its design.
  • It was initially supposed to be a temporary structure but became a permanent landmark due to its popularity.
